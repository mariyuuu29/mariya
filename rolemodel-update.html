My Role Model

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was known as the Missile Man of India. He played a major role in India's space and defense research. He was also the 11th President of India. He was loved by students all over the country. He believed in the power of dreams and education.

He was born in a small town called RMM in Tamil Nadu. Despite facing many difficulties, he never gave up. He studied physics and aerospace engineering. He worked with ISRO and DRDO. India loved him deeply.

Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action. This quote is from Dr. Kalam. His speeches were full of motivation and wisdom. He always emphasized education and hard work. He said, "You have to dream before your dreams come true."

He believed in simple living. His favorite subjects were science and math. He received the Padma Bhushan, Padma Vibhushan and Bharat Ratna. His life shows that even small town kids can do big things.

He always guided youth to do well.
