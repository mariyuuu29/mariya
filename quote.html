The sky at dusk tells a quiet story.
As the sun sets, golden light fades into soft pinks and deep purples,
painting the horizon with peace. Birds return to their nests, and the world slows down for a moment. It’s a time when silence feels comforting, like a warm blanket after a long day. In that stillness our thoughts often become clearer, reminding us to pause, breathe, and be present. Dusk is not just the end of a day—it’s a gentle reminder that endings can be beautiful too.
