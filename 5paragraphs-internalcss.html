Virat Kohli is one of the best batsmen in modern cricket. He has scored many centuries and led India as captain. His aggressive style and fitness are world-famous. Virat inspires millions of youngsters to play cricket. He is known as King Kohli by his fans.

MS Dhoni is known as Captain Cool of Indian cricket. He led India to win the 2007 T20 World Cup and 2011 World Cup. Dhoni is famous for his calm captaincy and finishing skills. His helicopter shot is very popular among fans. Even after retirement, he is deeply loved by all.

Sachin Tendulkar is called the God of Cricket. He has scored 100 international centuries in his career. Sachin played for India for over 20 years. He is respected worldwide for his humble nature. His records are an inspiration for every cricketer.

Rohit Sharma is known as the Hitman of Indian cricket. He holds the record for highest score in ODI - 264 runs. Rohit has hit many double centuries in ODIs. His timing and six-hitting ability are amazing. He is currently leading India in many matches.

Jasprit Bumrah is India's top fast bowler. He is famous for his deadly yorkers and accuracy. Bumrah performs very well in all three formats. His unique bowling action makes him special. He is a key player in India's bowling attack.
